LOL ... printers suck!!!

Publish date: Jan 19, 2017

Last year we visited some of our customers in EMEA to get some video footage about their Nitro Experience.

While we had our Creative Director in town (in Dublin), we also decided to shoot some material for some internal videos. Ooohhh … the fun we had …

  • Life at Nitro: The Nitro Family
  • Life Nitro: Our Dedication to Development
  • Life at Nitro: Our Diversity
  • Nitro: Our Vision for PDF
  • Life at Nitro: Our Leaders
  • Nitro: Our Thoughts on Printers

My favorite quote is … obviously … “Printers suck!!!". I am with Nitro now for 18 month, but have not configured the office printer yet and have no intentions to do so any time soon, because … … and the damn thing is most of the time in the state that most printers are all the time: Broken and/or not working for reasons that I am not able to comprehend.

Anyway … enjoy the show :).

PS: 4 weeks afterwards I channeled the Creative Director in me :).
