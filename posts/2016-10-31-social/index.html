Ooohhh boy ... twitterfeed is no more

2016-10-31

The other day I got and email that twitterfeed is no more (they recommend to use buffer or dlvr.it instead).

My social media setup was to post to Google+ and then use twitterfeed to cross-post to twitter and facebook.

With twitterfeed going out of business I am …

  • … going to retire my Google+ account
  • … going to use HootSuite to selectively post to twitter (business, technical, work) and facebook (private, personal)
  • … going to continue to use Flickr to publish pictures
  • … going to continue to use GitHub to host my blog (and will cross-post on medium and linked-in for visibility).

If you follow me on Google+, please feel free to do so on twitter :).
