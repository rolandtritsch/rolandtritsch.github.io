The corona virus ... and it's consequences

2020-03-30 Personal

The corona virus has a lot of weird side effects.

You suddenly find time to do things that you couldn’t do (or wouldn’t do) for month!!!

In my case I just updated/refreshed my blogging infrastructure …

  • switching from jekyll to hugo (more capable framework; good support for Emacs org-mode)
  • adding support for tags and categories
  • retiring www.tritsch.org and www.innolocity.org (replacing it with tedn.life (more on that in due course))

The result is aesthetically pleasing.
