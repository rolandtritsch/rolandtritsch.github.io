--- layout: post title: Effective Android - Using Apache CXF to access SOAP services date: '2009-09-22T07:28:00.003+01:00' author: Roland Tritsch tags: modified_time: '2012-09-16T19:53:30.202+01:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2124153373622490226.post-5597661726496460704 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.tritsch.org/2009/06/effective-android-using-apache-cxf-to.html --- A year ago I developed a small set of demos to show how to access SOAP services from the iPhone. To do this I used a very interesting feature of Apache CXF: The ability to generate a complete JavaScript client-side SOAP-stack on the fly.

I just finished an update/upgrade of this demo to show that it also works with the Android Phone. I also ported it to maven, means installing, building and running the demo is much easier now. The source code and the maven build environment is available on GitHub.

Enjoy :).
